Official CodeFoxSoft Launch!

By Nicholas Larkin Buchanan on July 29, 2025

After years of building systems, solving complex infrastructure puzzles, and pushing the boundaries of creative technology, I'm proud to announce the launch of CodeFoxSoft.com—a new chapter in my journey and a living portfolio of everything I've learned, built, and envisioned.

CodeFoxSoft isn’t just a website. It’s a reflection of my passion for secure, scalable web architecture, my obsession with privacy-respecting tools, and my love for blending backend mastery with creative expression. From TLS-secured servers to pixel-perfect game assets, every corner of this platform is handcrafted with intention and care.

This launch marks the convergence of years spent mastering Linux, Apache, DNS, cryptography, desktop development, and game design. It’s the result of countless late nights troubleshooting config files, rebuilding certs from scratch, and iterating through ideas until they felt just right. CodeFoxSoft is where all those pieces finally come together.

What You’ll Find

The site is designed as a modular ecosystem—featuring subdomains for development, assets, community, and analytics. You’ll find:

  • Game development projects and design insights
  • Desktop app development for privacy-first workflows
  • Custom scripts and automation tools for creators and developers
  • Infrastructure breakdowns and secure server configurations

Featured Project: Fox Run

One of the highlights of CodeFoxSoft is Fox Run, now live on the Google Play Store. It’s a fast-paced, pixel-perfect mobile game that blends retro charm with modern mechanics. Built from the ground up with performance and polish in mind, Fox Run showcases the kind of creative coding and design philosophy that drives everything I do.

From asset management to gameplay logic, every element of Fox Run reflects the same attention to detail and commitment to quality that powers CodeFoxSoft as a whole.

Why It Matters

CodeFoxSoft is more than a portfolio—it’s a declaration of independence from cloud lock-in, insecure defaults, and disposable tech. It’s built for creators, developers, and anyone who values control, transparency, and long-term maintainability. Every tool and post is designed to empower others to build with confidence and clarity.

This is just the beginning. I’ll be sharing updates, tools, and behind-the-scenes breakdowns as the platform evolves. Whether you're here to learn, collaborate, or just explore, I’m excited to have you along for the ride.

Welcome to CodeFoxSoft.com—where creativity meets infrastructure, and every byte tells a story.

About

Our Vision

CodeFoxSoft™ was founded with a single purpose: to innovate boldly and build systems that respect autonomy, privacy, and creativity. We believe technology should empower, not constrain. From apps and games to intelligent infrastructure, our mission is to craft experiences that are engaging, resilient, and future‑ready.

The Architect Behind the Code

Nicholas Buchanan's journey began in the 6th grade, self‑taught and driven by curiosity. He mastered QBasic before moving into object‑oriented programming, laying the foundation for decades of experience across languages and frameworks. His skills grew into graphics design, web design, logic design, and advanced programming — not through formal degrees, but through relentless practice and real‑world problem solving. While he is pursuing a BS today, his career proves that skill and vision often outpace credentials; after all, many of the greatest minds launched million‑dollar industries without degrees.

Under his belt is a two‑year internship with the Charles County Board of Education, where he completed Honors Java and AP Computer Science with distinction. That foundation expanded into mastery of Java, C#, C++, and scripting languages, enabling versatile development across platforms and cementing his reputation as a programmer who learns fast and builds with precision.

Nicholas's career has spanned diverse domains: managing high‑traffic game servers and online communities, contributing to FDIC Bank Fraud Investigations, and delivering customer service that sharpened his people‑first leadership. Each chapter added precision, empathy, and resilience to his toolkit. His entrepreneurial venture, FauxC, proved that vision and execution could coexist — acquiring, repairing, and reselling computer systems with expert craftsmanship.

From Biography to Brand

But CodeFoxSoft is more than one person's résumé. It is a company built on the belief that software should be modular, secure, and human‑centered. Every project we take on — whether a game, an app, or a system audit — is designed to deliver outcomes: faster performance, cleaner workflows, and experiences that feel alive. We don't just write code; we build ecosystems that respect the user's autonomy and scale with their ambitions.

Commissioned Work

CodeFoxSoft also takes on commissioned projects, delivering tailored solutions across a wide spectrum of needs:

  • Websites that are fast, secure, and designed for impact.
  • Windows and Mobile Applications built with precision and user‑centric design.
  • Games for Windows and Mobile Devices that blend storytelling, mechanics, and intelligent systems.
  • Custom LLMs (Large Language Models) designed for organizations that want AI tuned to their unique data and workflows.

Every commission is treated as a partnership: we listen, design, and deliver with the same meticulous care that defines our own flagship projects.

The Gaming Studio Dream

Our next frontier is gaming. CodeFoxSoft is laying the foundation for a studio that blends storytelling, mechanics, and intelligent systems into unforgettable experiences. Projects like Fox Run and Space Pong are just the beginning — proof of concept for a larger vision. We aim to create games that aren't just played, but lived: worlds that adapt, communities that thrive, and mechanics that challenge and inspire.

Why We Exist

We exist to push boundaries. To prove that a small studio can rival giants by being faster, sharper, and more human. To show that autonomy‑driven design can coexist with artistry. To build a future where every app, every game, and every system carries the mark of CodeFoxSoft: precision, resilience, and imagination. And above all, to prove that programmers are not obsolete in the age of AI — they are the leaders who will shape it.

2. Our Stance on User Data

Our philosophy is simple: we believe your data is your own. We do not collect or store your personal data for our use. Our applications are designed to function without needing to send your information to our servers.

  • Application Data: Any data generated or used by our applications (e.g., game saves, settings, user-created content) is stored locally on your own device. We do not have access to this information.
  • Online Experiences: For interactive online or multiplayer applications, it is necessary to transmit some data (such as a username or gameplay information) between users. This data is used solely to enable the gameplay experience and is not saved or used by us for any other purpose.
  • Contact Form: The only personal data we collect is the information you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name and email address, when you use the contact form on our website. This is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry.
  • Cookies: Our website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Any cookies used are strictly for essential site functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

Given that we collect minimal information, our use is limited. Information collected from the contact form is used solely to respond to your comments, questions, and provide customer service.

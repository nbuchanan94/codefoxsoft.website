Inside the CodeFoxSoft Dev Blog

Welcome to the central hub for all things CodeFoxSoft. This isn't just a place for announcements; it's a window into the development process of an indie studio. Here, Nicholas Larkin Buchanan shares the triumphs, challenges, and technical deep-dives involved in building modern games and web applications.

Whether you are an aspiring developer, a gaming enthusiast, or a client looking for web solutions, our blog offers valuable insights:

  • Development Logs (DevLogs): Follow the journey of games like Fox Run and Space Pong from concept art to final release. See how mechanics evolve and bugs get squashed.
  • Generative AI & Tech: Discover how we leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI tools to accelerate coding, generate assets, and push the boundaries of what a solo developer can achieve.
  • Web Development Tutorials: Learn about the technologies powering our sites, including HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript optimizations for SEO and performance.
  • Community Updates: Stay informed about beta testing opportunities, new feature rollouts, and community events. Your feedback shapes our future projects!

Stay tuned for regular updates. We believe in transparency and sharing knowledge with the wider developer community. Only by learning together can we build the future of software.

About CodeFoxSoft

Founded by Nicholas Buchanan, CodeFoxSoft is the culmination of a self‑taught programmer's journey from mastering QBasic in the 6th grade to building resilient apps, games, and intelligent systems. With nearly two decades of experience across Java, C#, C++, and design disciplines, Nicholas blends technical precision with bold vision. CodeFoxSoft delivers commissioned websites, mobile and Windows applications, immersive games, and custom LLMs — proving that in the age of AI, programmers aren't obsolete, they are the leaders shaping its future.

Why Play CodeFoxSoft Games?

At CodeFoxSoft, we believe in bringing the soul back to mobile gaming. In an era dominated by pay-to-win mechanics and intrusive advertisements, our mission is to create indie games that prioritize fun, creativity, and player experience above all else. Whether you are looking for a quick reflex challenge or a nostalgic trip down memory lane, our library has something for you.

Privacy Focused

We value your data. Our games are designed with privacy in mind, requiring minimal permissions and ensuring your personal information stays personal. Play with peace of mind knowing you aren't being tracked.

Pure Gameplay

From the endless running excitement of Fox Run to the brick-breaking strategy of Space Pong, our focus is on tight controls, satisfying mechanics, and "just one more try" addictiveness without the bloat.

Indie Heart

Every line of code and every pixel is crafted by Nicholas Larkin Buchanan. By downloading our games, you are directly supporting independent development and allowing us to dream bigger for future projects.

Optimized for Android

Our titles are rigorously tested to ensure smooth performance across a wide range of Android devices. Whether you rock the latest flagship or a trusty older phone, expect high frame rates and responsive touch controls.
